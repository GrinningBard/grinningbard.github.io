About the Book

Devaultus had no huge plans to become a hero. In fact, he can’t stand them. He’s just a stabby shapeshifting bard who tends to do things at his own pace. Until he gets pulled into playing the hero—not on purpose, mind you. But now people think he’s a savior. He’s never one to give up on a good tale, truth or lie. But will the world survive him building this one?

About J.A. Roggie

J.A. Roggie is the author of the upcoming epic dark fantasy series The Dark Deceiver Chronicles, a saga forged from fire, loss, and the relentless pursuit of hope in a shattered world.

Known for crafting flawed, fierce, and unforgettable characters, Roggie blends raw emotion with immersive world-building, balancing grit, heart, and just enough chaos to keep readers breathless.
