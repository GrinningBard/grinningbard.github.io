Welcome to the Shadows

I’m J.A. Roggie, the author behind The Book of Devaultus and other dark delights. I write tales soaked in shadow, sung by blades, and stitched together with mischief.

Who Is the Grinning Bard?

I’m not the hero — I just write their eulogies. My stories blend dark fantasy, chaotic characters, and poetic madness, led by Devaultus, Vex, and a bloody trail of gnomes with Irish accents.
