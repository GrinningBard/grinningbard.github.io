About J.A. Roggie

J.A. Roggie, better known as The Grinning Bard, weaves grim tales with sharp edges. Known for The Book of Devaultus and other dark fantasy works, Roggie pulls you into a world where nothing is safe — not even your soul.

Featured Book

In a world where storytelling is a weapon, one bard fights not just for truth — but for survival. The Book of Devaultus is a dark fantasy tale where betrayal tastes like wine and heroism wears a crooked grin.
